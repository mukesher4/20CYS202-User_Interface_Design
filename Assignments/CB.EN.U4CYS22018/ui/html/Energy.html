2 Types of Fission Bombs

  • There were 2 types of bombs developed: gun type and implosion type.
  • In a gun-type structure, a piece of uranium-238 is propelled into another piece by a convention explosive that allows the uranium to reach criticallity, detonating the device
  • In an implosion type device, the same used in trinity, the plutonium core with a neutron initiator in the middle is compressed by an external sphere of explosives. This compression activates the neutron source and helps the core achieve criticality.
